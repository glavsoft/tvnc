There are three public TightVNC mailing lists:

Mailing List Description
TightVNC Announcements This low-traffic mailing list includes announcements about important events such as new TightVNC releases or other major news related to the TightVNC project. Only TightVNC administrators can post messages to this list.
TightVNC List This list is for general discussions about TightVNC. Also, it's a good place to ask questions on TightVNC installation and usage. All subscribers are allowed to post messages (send messages to vnc-tight-list@lists.sourceforge.net).
TightVNC Development This list is devoted to the TightVNC development process. It is for TightVNC developers, and everyone interested in the development. All subscribers are allowed to post messages (send messages to vnc-tight-devel@lists.sourceforge.net).

How to Subscribe

  1. Choose a link from the table above, to go to the Web page of a corresponding mailing list.
  2. Carefully read and follow the instructions in the "Subscribing..." section of that page.

How to Unsubscribe

  1. Choose a link from the table above, to go to the Web page of a corresponding mailing list.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of that page, and enter your e-mail address in the text field near the "Edit Options" button.
  3. Press the "Edit Options" button. That will move you to the mailing list configuration page.
  4. Read and follow the instructions in the "Unsubscribing..." section of that page.

Important note: Unsubscribe requests sent to mailing lists or to list administrators will be silently ignored. Please unsubscribe from the mailing lists using the procedure described above.

How to Post Messages

  • Before you post, make sure you are subscribed to the list, and verify that your subscription and posting addresses are exactly the same. Otherwise, your messages will be rejected.
  • Choose a good text for the Subject header of the message. Please do not use subjects like "help" or "problem". Choose informative ones instead.
  • To post a message to all the members of a particular list, send e-mail to one of the addresses from the table on the top of this page.
  • When answering a message in the mailing list, please use "Reply to all" feature of your e-mail reader, to make sure a copy of the answer will be delivered back to the mailing list, not just to the sender.
  • When quoting original messages in your answers, please write your comments below the quoted paragraphs.

Searching List Archives

  1. Choose one of these links to go to the mailing list archives: [ TightVNC List | Development | Announcements ]
  2. Enter a search phrase into the text field near the "Search" button, at the upper right corner of the page.
  3. Press the "Search" button.
